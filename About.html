Greater Noida Institute of Technology (GNIOT), established in 2001, is one of the premier institutions in India offering high-quality education in Engineering, Management, Computer Applications, and related fields.

GNIOT is committed to nurturing talent and transforming students into industry-ready professionals. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, highly qualified faculty, and strong industry partnerships, the institute provides an environment that encourages innovation, leadership, and holistic development.

Over the years, GNIOT has earned a reputation for academic excellence, research initiatives, and outstanding placement records. The institute aims to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values to excel in their chosen careers and contribute positively to society.
